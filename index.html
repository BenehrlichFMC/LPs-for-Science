What We Do

At LPs for Science, we are a collective of forward-thinking capital allocators committed to catalyzing the launch and growth of deeply technical venture funds. Our mission is to bridge the gap between innovative scientific talent and the capital needed to bring groundbreaking ideas to life.

Key aspects of our approach:

  • Active Deployment: All our members are actively deploying capital, ensuring real-time market insights and opportunities.
  • Inclusive Approach: We believe in potential. No fund is too early in its lifecycle, and no scientist is too unproven to apply.
  • Direct Engagement: We personally reach out to applicants whose visions align with our mission and show promise.

Your groundbreaking idea could be the key to solving some of the world's most pressing challenges. Don't let lack of capital hold you back.

Our Mission

LPs for Science is dedicated to addressing the major challenges facing our society through strategic investment in scientific and technological innovation. We focus on four critical areas:

  • Safe AI development
  • Climate disaster mitigation
  • Energy transition
  • Solving complex diseases

Our approach is rooted in the belief that these complex issues can only be solved through the invention of new, commercially viable technologies.

We aim to:

  1. Provide funding, feedback, and mentorship to the most scientifically or technically sound General Partners (GPs)
  2. Increase the amount of capital allocated to cutting-edge scientific and technological fields
  3. Connect Limited Partners (LPs) with GPs who focus on investing in scientific founders and technologies
  4. Foster a community of investors committed to driving meaningful technological progress

By bridging the gap between capital and groundbreaking scientific ventures, we strive to accelerate the development of solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.
